About OM Tokens

OM Tokens are the entry pieces for investors to become OM community members and get involved with guiding MANTRA DAO. Holders of OM Token receive voting rights which can be used to participate in governance polls and proposals, enabling the community to collectively steer strategic decisions and evolution of the platform. OM Tokens also provide access to rewards programs and involvement across other key initiatives and ecosystem developments.

How to stake

Stake

Select your preferred chain and enter the amount of tokens you want to lock in the staking contract

Earn daily rewards

Once staked, you begin earning staking rewards in proportion to the amount staked

How to unstake

Unstake

Select the amount of tokens to unstake and confirm transaction in your wallet. This starts the unbonding period. See details below.

Claim

After the unbonding period ends, you can claim your original staked tokens and the accumulated staking rewards. When you choose to claim and confirm the transaction in your wallet, the tokens are returned back to your wallet.

Unstaking Fees

When you select 'Unstake', your tokens will go through an unbonding period before they can be claimed. There are 2 methods available to unbond depending on you requirement.
